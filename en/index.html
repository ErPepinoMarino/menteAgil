What is Sharp Mind?

Sharp Mind is a free brain games app designed to train memory, logic, and concentration. It includes Sudoku, Nonograms, and other cognitive challenges for all ages.

Two Versions, Same Experience

Both versions include exactly the same games and features. The paid version removes ads to support the app development.
